Integrations

Drop in credentials for each external system; once saved they start working automatically — the backend reads from these records (cached for ~60 s per Function instance) and uses them in place of any env-var fallback. Secrets are stored in your tenant's Cosmos container, gated by your Entra sign-in. Click Show on any masked field to reveal it; click Test to ping the provider.
